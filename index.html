💣 Minesweeper

Built on Boolean Algebra — CIA-1

How to play

Left click Reveal a cell
Right click Place or remove a flag 🚩

The number on a revealed cell = count of bombs in its 8 neighbours (sum of boolean isMine values).

Flag all bombs. Reveal every safe cell to win. Click a bomb = game over.

At game over:
🟢 Green flag = correct (bomb was there, ghost bomb shown behind)
🔴 Red flag = wrong (neighbour count shown behind the flag)
🟡 Amber = unfound bomb you missed
Boolean algebra used:
isMine, isRevealed, isFlagged → each is 0 or 1
Guard: isRevealed | isFlagged (OR)
Win: isMine | isRevealed = 1 for ALL cells (AND)
Flag toggle: isFlagged ^= 1 (XOR)
Mine count: sum of 8 boolean isMine neighbours
